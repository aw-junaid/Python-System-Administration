## 🛠️ Project-Specific Channels

- **Bug Reports / Feature Requests:** [Open an Issue](https://github.com/aw-junaid/Python-System-Administration/issues/new)
- **Code Contributions:** [Open a Pull Request](https://github.com/aw-junaid/Python-System-Administration/pulls) — see contribution guidelines in [README.md](./README.md#-contributing)
- **Security Reports:** Follow the private disclosure process in [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md) — please do not report vulnerabilities publicly
- **Discussions & Community Q&A:** [Discord Server](https://discord.gg/Neddn8gPqY)

## ⏱️ Response Times

- **Email:** Typically within 2–5 business days
- **GitHub Issues/PRs:** Reviewed regularly; response time varies with volume
- **Discord:** Fastest for casual/community questions, though not guaranteed real-time
- **Security reports:** Acknowledged within 3 business days per [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md)

> [!TIP]
> For the quickest response on technical questions about this repository specifically, opening a GitHub Issue is usually faster than email or social media, since it's searchable and helps other users with the same question.
